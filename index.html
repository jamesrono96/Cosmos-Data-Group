Who We Are

Professional Data Intelligence & Advisory Firm

Cosmos Data Group is a professional services and data intelligence firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We deliver integrated solutions across data science, research, strategic advisory, tax consulting, risk management, and digital intelligence — serving the private sector, public sector, development organisations, and civil society.

Founded by professionals with expertise in economics, data science, and public policy, we combine rigorous analytical methods with practical East African operating knowledge.
